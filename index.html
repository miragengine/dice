previous projects

silence was one of my earliest and most ambitious projects in the Roblox world. It was an external cheat that broke new ground with its camlock feature, following in the footsteps of projects like Celex and Severe. Silence also introduced a smooth ESP, making it one of the best visuals in the community at the time. Unfortunately, the source code was leaked, marking the end of Silence as we knew it.

after Silence, i built mirage, a new and more advanced roblox external. mirage is designed to be adaptable, supporting popular games like Phantom Forces, Blade Ball, and Bad Business, as well as all Roblox R6 and R15 games. it's got features like silent aim and a highly smooth aimbot, along with support for Da Hood and its various copies. mirage is a fresh start — more robust, versatile, and secure.

the journey doesn’t stop at roblox. i’m working on a fortnite external cheat under the Mirage brand, which will bring a new level of external enhancement to the game. stay tuned!
